Profile

As a Biomedical Engineer with a Ph.D. and experience in dynamic fields of biomedical engineering, I am a highly organized and dependable candidate with a proven track record of managing multiple priorities with a positive attitude. I am willing to take on added responsibilities to meet team goals and am known for my energetic and critical-thinking approach to problem-solving. I am self-motivated and astute, always striving for excellence in my work.

  • Selected for the Ph.D. program under QIP at the Centre for Biomedical Engineering, IIT Delhi.
  • Invited to Technical University of Munich (TUM) for technical discussions in an Indo-German collaborative project.
  • Received a travel grant of ₹1,50,000 to attend an international conference under research promotion schemes.
  • Youngest faculty to be granted patents in the College of Engineering, Osmania University.
